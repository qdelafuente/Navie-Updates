Before you start

Navie AI needs your permission to work

What Navie sends to the AI model

  • Names and IDs of your enrolled courses
  • Assignment titles and due dates from your gradebook
  • Academic calendar events
  • Syllabus content and course announcements
  • Your conversation messages during the session

What Navie NEVER sends to the AI

  • Your name, email address or student ID
  • Your Blackboard password or login credentials

Data is processed by OpenRouter / Anthropic (USA) under a Data Processing Agreement. It is never used to train AI models.

Read the full Privacy Policy
